Goshen Royal School About-Us

Welcome to Goshen Royal School

At Goshen Royal School, we extend a warm welcome to every parent, guardian, and student who joins our family. We believe education is more than just passing exams — it's about developing confident, capable, and compassionate individuals who are ready to make a positive impact in the world.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a vibrant learning community offering Pre-school, Primary, Junior High School, and Remedial (WASSCE) classes. Every stage of learning is designed to provide a strong academic foundation, encourage curiosity, and inspire lifelong learning.

Goshen Royal School is committed to combining academic excellence with personal growth. Our curriculum goes beyond textbooks, blending core subjects with enriching programs such as:

  • Abacus: This is a program that teaches children how to solve math problems fast using a special tool called an abacus. It helps your child's brain become quicker at thinking about numbers.
  • Cadet Training: Here, children learn to be responsible and disciplined. They are taught to be leaders and to work well with others as a team. This training helps them grow into strong, respectful adults.
  • Cultural Activities: We teach children to be proud of their culture and community. They learn about our traditions, our history, and how to respect everyone around them. This helps them feel like they belong and love where they come from.
  • Art & Craft: In these classes, children use their hands to create new things. This helps them be more creative, use their imagination, and become better at solving problems.

Our team of dedicated and experienced educators work closely with students, ensuring every child receives personalized attention and encouragement to reach their full potential. We also place a strong emphasis on moral values and character formation, helping to shape responsible and respectful citizens.

Our Core Values

At the heart of Goshen Royal School are the values that guide everything we do

  • Integrity

  • Team Work

    We encourage Working together to achieve common goals.

  • Creativity

    Inspiring imagination and innovative thinking.

  • Discipline

    We encourage self-control, responsibility, and focus.

  • Respect

    We value self, others, and our diverse community.

  • Compassion

  • Excellence

  • Perseverance
