Why Choose Goshen Royal School for Your Child’s Education

At Goshen Royal School, we believe every child is gifted with unique potential, and our mission is to nurture that potential into excellence.

We also prioritize moral values, character development, and a supportive learning environment where every child feels valued and confident.

With dedicated teachers, modern teaching methods, and a caring community, Goshen Royal School is more than a place of learning — it’s a home where your child can grow academically, socially, and emotionally to become a responsible, future-ready leader.

Our Special Offerings

We provide GES curriculums. Aside that, we also streamline a variety of special programs which aids to bring out the craft in children.

Abacus

This is a program that teaches children how to solve math problems fast using a special tool called an abacus. It helps your child's brain become quicker at thinking about numbers.

Cadet Training

Here, children learn to be responsible and disciplined. They are taught to be leaders and to work well with others as a team. This training helps them grow into strong, respectful adults..

Cultural Activities

We teach children to be proud of their culture and community. They learn about our traditions, our history, and how to respect everyone around them. This helps them feel like they belong and love where they come from.

Art & Craft

In these classes, children use their hands to create new things. This helps them be more creative, use their imagination, and become better at solving problems.
